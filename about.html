This position has improved my skills with technology, as I worked heavily with Microsoft Office applications. It also allowed me to broaden my soft skills, which includes communication, creativity, problem solving, flexibility, and detail orientation.

I am interested in artificial intelligence, machine learning, marketing, and psychology.
